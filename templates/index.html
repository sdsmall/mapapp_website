Summary of functionality
Open up the homescreen to find buttons corresponding to categories of points of interest. Clicking on one of these buttons will take you to a Google map with locations for the chosen item relative to your location on campus. Click on a marker to view details in a pop-up containing text and buttons. Functional buttons within pop-up can open a hyperlink of interest or dial calls to relevant phone numbers.

What is the target audience?
College students!
